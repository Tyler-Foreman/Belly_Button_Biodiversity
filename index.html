Demographic Info

In this scientific study, the team looked to discover and document bacterial species that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Our lab has partnered with Impossible Beef, a food startup to research candidate species, for this study. The human body has thousands of different kinds of bacteria, and differennt body parts will house different species.

We hypothesize that the ideal bacteria to make synthetic beef, may be found in someones belly button. To properly test our hypothesis, we havve sampled thousands of particpiants navels to identify bacterial specis that colonize our belly buttons. Each study participant is anonymous and has been assigned an ID number.

The bubble chart above shows a bubble for each bacteria species found. The size of the bubble corerelates to the amount of bacteria found in the sample from each study participant.
